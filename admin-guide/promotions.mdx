## Overview

Admins control homepage promotions:
- Banner carousel
- Small cover features
- Pinned stories
- Editor's picks

## Accessing Promotions

Navigate to **จัดการเนื้อหา > โปรโมท (Content > Promote)**.

## Promotion Types

### Banner Promotions
Top carousel on homepage:
- Maximum visibility
- Auto-rotating display
- Typically 3-5 active slots

### Small Cover Promotions
Featured covers section:
- Horizontal scroll
- Below main banner
- Multiple slots

### Pinned Stories
Editor's pick section:
- "Recommended" style display
- Quality curation
- Persistent placement

## Admin-Picked Promotions

### Creating a Promotion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Type">
    Choose Banner, Small Cover, or Pinned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Comic">
    Search and select the comic to feature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Duration">
    Choose start and end dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Promotion becomes active at start time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Active Promotions
- View currently active promotions
- Edit duration
- Remove if needed
- Reorder if applicable

## Bidding Winners

Creators can bid for promotion slots. Admins manage:
- Review winning bids
- Approve placement
- Handle disputes
- Process payments

### Bidding Period Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Period">
    Set bidding start and end times.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Bids">
    Watch incoming bids during period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close Bidding">
    Period ends automatically or manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Winners">
    Approve and activate winning placements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Promotion Guidelines

### What to Promote
- Quality content
- Active comics with regular updates
- Content matching platform standards
- Variety of genres

### What to Avoid
- Abandoned comics
- Policy-violating content
- Duplicate promotions
- Favoritism

## Scheduling Promotions

Plan promotions ahead:
- Schedule in advance
- Coordinate with events
- Balance different creators
- Seasonal themes

## Promotion Analytics

Track promotion effectiveness:
- Views during promotion
- Click-through rates
- Purchase increases
- Creator feedback

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Rotate Content">
    Don't keep the same promotions forever. Fresh content engages users.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quality Over Connections">
    Promote based on content quality, not personal relationships.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Balance Genres">
    Feature diverse content types and genres.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Support New Creators">
    Give new quality creators visibility opportunities.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
