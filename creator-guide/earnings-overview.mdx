## How Earnings Work

When readers purchase your chapters, you earn revenue:

```
Reader Purchase
      ↓
Platform Processes
      ↓
Revenue Split: Creator (~85%) + Platform (~15%)
      ↓
Accumulated in Your Account
      ↓
Payout When Threshold Met
```

## Revenue Split

| Party | Percentage |
|-------|------------|
| **Creator** | ~85% |
| **Platform** | ~15% |

<Note>
Exact percentages may vary based on your creator agreement. Check your terms for specific rates.
</Note>

## Understanding Your Revenue

### Per-Chapter Earnings
For each chapter purchase:
```
Chapter Price: 5 coins
Coin Value: ~฿0.89
Gross Revenue: 5 × ฿0.89 = ฿4.45
Your Share (85%): ฿3.78
```

### Cumulative Earnings
All chapter purchases accumulate to your pending balance.

More readers = more potential purchasers

- Cross-promote with other creators

### Optimize Pricing

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do I earn from a purchase?">
    Earnings are credited immediately when a reader purchases your chapter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see who purchased my chapters?">
    You see transaction counts but buyer details are anonymized for privacy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to earnings if I delete a comic?">
    Earned revenue remains yours, but future purchases from deleted content stop.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a maximum I can earn?">
    No cap on earnings. Your revenue scales with your readership and content.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
