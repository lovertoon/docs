## What Are Assistants?

Assistants are team members you invite to help manage your creator account. They can:
- Upload and manage chapters
- Edit comic information
- Handle day-to-day content tasks

<Info>
Assistants have limited permissions. They can manage content but cannot access finances or account settings.
</Info>

## Accessing Assistants

From your dashboard, go to **ผู้ช่วย (Assistants)**.

## Inviting an Assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Assistants">
    Navigate to Assistants section in dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **Invite Assistant** or **+ Add**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Enter the email of the person you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Click **Send** to send the invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Acceptance">
    The invitee receives an email and must accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invitation Process

### For Creators
1. Send invitation via email
2. Track pending invitations
3. Cancel if needed before acceptance
4. Manage after acceptance

### For Invitees
1. Receive email notification
2. Click link to accept
3. Log in (or create account)
4. Get assistant access to creator's dashboard

## Assistant Permissions

### What Assistants CAN Do
- View comic list
- Create and edit chapters
- Upload images
- Edit comic details
- View comments

### What Assistants CANNOT Do
- Access earnings or payouts
- Delete comics
- Manage billing
- Invite other assistants
- Change account settings

<Warning>
Choose assistants carefully. They have significant content control over your comics.
</Warning>

## Managing Assistants

### View Current Assistants
See all your assistants:
- Name and email
- When joined
- Status (active/pending)

### Removing an Assistant
To revoke assistant access:
1. Find the assistant in the list
2. Click **Remove** or the X icon
3. Confirm removal
4. Their access is immediately revoked

### Assistant Limits
You may have limits on how many assistants you can have. Check your creator tier.

## Assistant Dashboard

### What Assistants See
Assistants access a limited version of your dashboard:
- Comics section
- Chapter management
- Basic comic editing
- Your creator name (as context)

### Limited Navigation
Assistants don't see:
- Earnings/Payouts
- Billing
- Marketing tools
- Assistant management

## For Assistants

### Accepting an Invitation
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    Look for invitation email from Lovertoon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Accept Link">
    Follow the link in the email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In or Register">
    Sign in with existing account or create new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    You now have assistant access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Working as an Assistant
- Your role is clearly labeled
- You work under the creator's brand
- Creator sees all your changes
- You cannot access your own creator features (if any)

### Leaving a Creator Team
Assistants can leave voluntarily:
1. Go to dashboard
2. Find "Leave Team" option
3. Confirm departure
4. Access is removed

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can assistants see my earnings?">
    No, financial information is private to the creator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I be an assistant for multiple creators?">
    Yes, one account can be an assistant for multiple creators.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if an assistant goes rogue?">
    Remove them immediately. Review any changes they made. Contact support if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do assistants earn anything?">
    No, assistants don't receive platform revenue. Payment arrangements are private between creator and assistant.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
