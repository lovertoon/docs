## Accessing Chapters

To manage chapters for a comic:
1. Go to **Comics** in dashboard
2. Click on a comic
3. View the **Chapters** section

## Adding a New Chapter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Comic">
    Open the comic you want to add a chapter to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Chapter">
    Click **Add Chapter** or **+ New Chapter**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in chapter information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Content">
    Add images (for comics) or text (for novels).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Price">
    Choose free or set coin price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Save and publish the chapter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Chapter Information

### Required Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Chapter title |
| **Order** | Chapter sequence number |
| **Content** | Images or text (depending on comic type) |

### Optional Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Order Name** | Custom display name (e.g., "Prologue") |
| **Description** | Chapter summary |
| **Cover Image** | Chapter-specific thumbnail |
| **Price** | Coin cost (0 = free) |

## Chapter Ordering

### Automatic Order
Chapters are automatically ordered by their **Order** field.

### Custom Order Names
Use **Order Name** for special chapters:
- "Prologue"
- "Epilogue"
- "Side Story 1"
- "Special Chapter"

If Order Name is set, it displays as "Episode [Order Name]" instead of "Episode [Order]".

## Uploading Comic Content

### Image Upload
For **Comic** type content:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Upload Area">
    Click the image upload zone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Images">
    Choose multiple images at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange Order">
    Drag to reorder if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Unwanted">
    Click X to remove any image.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Image Specifications

| Aspect | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Format** | JPG, PNG, WebP |
| **Width** | 800-1200 pixels recommended |
| **File Size** | Under 2MB per image |
| **Color Mode** | RGB |

<Tip>
Optimize your images for web. Large files slow down loading and frustrate readers.
</Tip>

## Uploading Novel Content

For **Novel** type content:
- Use the text editor to write/paste content
- Basic formatting supported (bold, italic, etc.)
- Chapters can be any length

## Chapter Pricing

### Free Chapters
Set **Amount** to 0 (zero) to make a chapter free.

### Paid Chapters
Enter the coin amount readers must pay.

<Info>
See [Chapter Pricing](/creator-guide/chapter-pricing) for pricing strategies.
</Info>

## Chapter Status

Chapters can be:
- **Published** - Visible to readers
- **Draft** - Not yet published (if supported)

## Editing Chapters

To edit an existing chapter:
1. Navigate to the comic
2. Find the chapter in the list
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make changes
5. Save

### What Can Be Edited
- Title and description
- Content (images/text)
- Price (affects new purchases only)
- Cover image
- Order

<Warning>
Changing chapter order may confuse readers if they're mid-series. Use carefully.
</Warning>

## Deleting Chapters

<Warning>
Deleting a chapter:
- Removes all content permanently
- Removes associated comments
- Does NOT refund purchasers
- Cannot be undone
</Warning>

## Chapter Cover Images

Optional cover images:
- Appear in chapter lists
- Help identify chapters visually
- Recommended: Key scene from the chapter

## Bulk Operations

For efficiency, consider:
- Preparing all images before uploading
- Writing descriptions in advance
- Scheduling regular publication times

## Reader Experience

When readers view chapters:
- Free chapters are accessible immediately
- Paid chapters show purchase prompt
- Purchase unlocks permanent access
- Progress is tracked automatically

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Consistent Chapter Length">
    Maintain similar chapter lengths for consistent reader experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quality Over Quantity">
    Better to publish one great chapter than multiple mediocre ones.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Clear Chapter Titles">
    Descriptive titles help readers find specific chapters later.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="First Chapters Free">
    Consider making early chapters free to hook new readers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Optimize Images">
    Compress images without losing quality for faster loading.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
