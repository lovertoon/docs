## Overview

As a creator, you can publish:
- **Comics (การ์ตูน)** - Visual stories with images
- **Novels (นิยาย)** - Text-based stories

This guide covers the process for creating either type.

## Creating a New Comic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Comics Page">
    In your dashboard, click **การ์ตูน (Comics)** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create New">
    Click the **Create New Comic** or **+ New** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Comic Details">
    Enter all required information (see below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Images">
    Add cover image and optional banner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Categories">
    Choose relevant genre categories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** or **Create** to publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Comic Information

### Required Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Thai title of your comic |
| **Slug** | URL-friendly identifier (auto-generated from name) |
| **Description** | Synopsis/summary of your comic |
| **Content Type** | Comic or Novel |
| **Cover Image** | Main cover image |

### Optional Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **English Name** | English title (for international readers) |
| **Banner Image** | Wide banner for detail page |
| **Target Audience** | Male, Female, BL/GL, or All |
| **Status** | Ongoing, Completed, Hiatus, Dropped |
| **Adult Content** | Mark if 18+ content |

## Image Requirements

### Cover Image
- **Purpose**: Main thumbnail shown everywhere
- **Recommended ratio**: 5:7 (portrait)
- **Minimum resolution**: 500x700 pixels
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, WebP
- **File size**: Under 5MB

### Banner Image (Optional)
- **Purpose**: Background on detail page
- **Recommended ratio**: 16:9 (landscape)
- **Minimum resolution**: 1920x1080 pixels
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, WebP
- **File size**: Under 10MB

<Tip>
High-quality cover images attract more readers! Invest time in creating eye-catching covers.
</Tip>

## Target Audience

| Audience | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **All** | General audience, suitable for everyone |
| **Male** | Content primarily appealing to male readers |
| **Female** | Content primarily appealing to female readers |
| **BL/GL** | Boys' Love or Girls' Love content |

## Status Settings

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Ongoing** | Actively publishing new chapters |
| **Completed** | Story is finished |
| **Hiatus** | Temporarily paused |
| **Dropped** | Discontinued |

<Warning>
Keep your status updated! Readers appreciate knowing when to expect updates.
</Warning>

## Adult Content

If your comic contains adult content:
1. Check the **Adult/18+** option
2. Your comic will only appear to users who have enabled adult content
3. A red 18+ badge will be displayed

## After Creation

Once your comic is created:
- It appears in your Comics list
- It's visible on the platform (with no chapters yet)
- You can start adding chapters
- Edit details anytime

## Editing Comics

To edit an existing comic:
1. Go to **Comics** in dashboard
2. Find the comic in the list
3. Click **Edit** or the pencil icon
4. Update information
5. Save changes

## Deleting Comics

<Warning>
Deleting a comic is permanent and removes all associated chapters, comments, and purchase history. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>
