## What is Promotion Bidding?

Promotion Bidding is a system where creators compete for premium visibility slots on the Lovertoon homepage:
- Banner carousel slots
- Small cover placements
- Pinned story positions

Winners get their comics featured prominently.

## Available Slots

### Banner Slots
Top carousel on homepage:
- Maximum visibility
- Rotating display
- Premium placement

### Small Cover Slots
Featured covers section:
- Horizontal scroll display
- Below the main banner
- Good visibility

### Pinned Story Slots
Featured in recommendations:
- "Editor's Pick" style display
- Persistent placement
- Quality association

## How Bidding Works

### Weekly Bidding Period
<Steps>
  <Step title="Bidding Opens">
    New bidding period starts (typically weekly).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Bids">
    Choose slot type and submit your bid amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bidding Closes">
    Period ends at specified time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Winners Selected">
    Highest bidders win slots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Placement Period">
    Winners' comics are featured.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bid Types
- **Single bid**: One-time submission
- **Maximum bid**: Auto-increase up to your limit

## Winning & Losing

### If You Win
- Payment is processed
- Your comic appears in the slot
- Featured for the placement period
- You receive notifications

### If You Lose
- No payment charged
- Comic doesn't get featured
- Can try again next period
- Consider adjusting strategy

## Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:
- Bidding period start
- Outbid alerts
- Win/loss results
- Placement start/end

The sidebar shows unread notifications with a badge.

## Payment

### When Charged
- Only charged if you win
- Payment processed after bidding closes
- Deducted from your account balance or charged directly

### Refunds
- Losing bids are not charged
- Winning bids are final

- As specified in bidding terms

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I modify my bid after submitting?">
    Typically you can increase but not decrease. Check current period rules.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if there are ties?">
    Tie-breaking rules apply (usually first bidder wins or both featured).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I bid on multiple slots?">
    Yes, you can bid on different slot types with different comics.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How competitive is bidding?">
    Varies by period. Sometimes slots go for minimal bids.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
