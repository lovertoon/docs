## Application Process

Becoming a Lovertoon creator requires an application review. Here's how it works:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Log in to your Lovertoon account (create one if needed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Apply Page">
    Navigate to `/join-creator` or click "Become a Creator" in the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out Application">
    Complete the application form with required information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Review and submit your application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Review">
    Our team reviews applications within 3-5 business days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Approved">
    Receive approval notification and access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application Form

### Required Information

| Field | Description | Requirements |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Username** | Your unique creator identifier | Letters (a-z, A-Z), numbers (0-9), underscore (_) only |
| **Display Name** | Name shown to readers | Your public creator name |
| **Bio** | About yourself | Describe your experience and style |
| **Reason Message** | Why you want to be a creator | Explain your motivation |
| **Sample Work** | Description of your work | Describe your comics/novels |
| **Portfolio Links** | External links | Optional social media/portfolio URLs |

### Username Guidelines

Your username:
- Becomes your profile URL (`/creator/username`)
- Cannot be changed after approval
- Must be unique on the platform
- Only supports: a-z, A-Z, 0-9, underscore (_)

<Tip>
Choose a username that's professional and memorable. It represents your brand!
</Tip>

### Writing Your Bio

A good bio includes:
- Your creative background
- Types of content you create
- Your artistic style
- What readers can expect

### Reason Message

Explain:
- Why you want to publish on Lovertoon
- Your goals as a creator
- What makes your work unique
- Your commitment to quality

### Sample Work

Describe:
- Your best work
- Genre and style
- Target audience
- Any achievements or recognition

### Portfolio Links

Add links to:
- Personal website
- DeviantArt, Pixiv, etc.
- Social media (Twitter, Instagram)
- Other platforms where you publish

<Info>
Portfolio links help reviewers assess your work quality. Include your best showcases!
</Info>

## Application Status

### Pending
Your application is in review. Wait for our team to process it.

### Approved
Congratulations! You can now access the Creator Dashboard.

### Declined
Your application wasn't approved. You'll receive feedback and can reapply.

## Checking Application Status

After submitting:
1. Return to `/join-creator`
2. See your application status
3. View submitted information
4. Check for approval/decline message

## If Declined

Don't be discouraged! You can:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Feedback">
    Check the decline reason provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Improve Your Application">
    Address the feedback in your portfolio or bio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reapply">
    Submit a new application with improvements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Common Decline Reasons

| Reason | How to Address |
|--------|----------------|
| **Insufficient portfolio** | Add more work samples to external links |
| **Unclear work samples** | Provide clearer descriptions of your work |
| **Generic application** | Make your bio and reasons more personal |
| **Quality concerns** | Improve your craft and reapply later |

## Important Notes

### Creator Agreement
By applying, you agree to:
- Platform terms of service
- Creator guidelines
- Revenue sharing terms
- Content policies

### Responsibilities
As a creator, you're expected to:
- Publish original content only
- Maintain content quality
- Engage with your readers
- Follow platform rules

<Warning>
Providing false information in your application may result in immediate rejection and potential account suspension.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does approval take?">
    Typically 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email notification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my username later?">
    No, usernames are permanent after approval. Choose carefully!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to have published work already?">
    No, but you should be able to describe your planned content and demonstrate creative ability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I apply with a team account?">
    Individual accounts only. One person per creator account. You can add assistants later.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I'm already a creator on another platform?">
    Great! Include your other platform profiles in portfolio links.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
