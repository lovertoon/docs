## What is Creator PASS?

Creator PASS is a promotional program that features your comics as **Hot Stories** on the Lovertoon homepage. When your comic is a Hot Story:
- It appears prominently on the homepage
- PASS holders get 50% off on your chapters
- More readers discover your work

## How It Works

```
Creator Subscribes to Creator PASS
            ↓
Selects Comic to Promote
            ↓
Comic Appears as "Hot Story"
            ↓
Increased Visibility + PASS Discounts
            ↓
More Readers & Revenue
```

## Accessing Creator PASS

From your dashboard, go to **Creator PASS** in the Marketing section.

## Promoting a Comic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Creator PASS">
    Navigate to the Creator PASS section in dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Comic">
    Choose which comic to promote as Hot Story.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Duration">
    Choose promotion period (e.g., 24 hours, 48 hours).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay Fee">
    Complete payment for the promotion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activation">
    Your comic becomes a Hot Story immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Promotion Duration

| Duration | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **24 hours** | Testing the waters, quick promotions |
| **48 hours** | Standard promotions |
| **Extended** | Major launches, sustained campaigns |

## Hot Story Display

When your comic is a Hot Story:

### On Homepage
- Appears in "Hot Stories" section
- Shows "HOT" badge
- Displays countdown timer
- 50% discount visible to PASS holders

### On Comic Page
- "Hot Story" indicator
- Discounted prices for PASS users
- Promotion end time

## Pricing & Payments

<Note>
Creator PASS promotion fees vary. Check your dashboard for current pricing.
</Note>

Payment options:
- Direct payment
- Coin balance deduction
- Platform credit

## PASS Holder Discount

When readers with active PASS purchase your Hot Story chapters:
- They pay 50% of your set price
- You still receive your revenue share
- Platform subsidizes the difference

<Info>
The discount doesn't reduce your earnings. It's an incentive paid by the PASS program to drive traffic.
</Info>

## Managing Active Promotions

### Viewing Status
See your active Hot Story promotions:
- Current promoted comics
- Time remaining
- Performance metrics

### Multiple Promotions
You can have multiple comics as Hot Stories simultaneously (if budget allows).

## Post-Promotion

After the promotion ends:
- Comic returns to normal status
- Chapters return to full price for all users
- Any momentum built continues

### Leveraging Momentum
After Hot Story period:
- Engage with new readers
- Encourage favorites
- Maintain chapter release schedule

## Common Questions
