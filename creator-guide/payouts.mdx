## Payout Overview

When your pending balance reaches the minimum threshold, you can request a payout to receive your earnings.

## Payout Requirements

### Minimum Threshold
You must accumulate a minimum balance before requesting payout:
- Minimum: **฿500** (or equivalent)
- This ensures efficient processing

### Account Requirements
- Bank account information on file
- Valid identification verified
- Active creator status

## Requesting a Payout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Balance">
    Go to **รายได้ (Earnings)** and verify pending balance meets minimum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Payouts">
    Click **Request Payout** or navigate to payout section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Specify how much to withdraw (up to your available balance).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Details">
    Confirm your bank account information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Click **Request** to submit for processing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payout Methods

### Bank Transfer
Direct transfer to your Thai bank account:
- Most common method
- Usually 3-5 business days
- Requires verified bank information

<Note>
Additional payout methods may be available. Check your dashboard for current options.
</Note>

## Payout Schedule

### Request Processing
| Phase | Timeframe |
|-------|-----------|
| **Submitted** | Your request is received |
| **Scheduled** | Queued for processing |
| **Processing** | Being transferred |
| **Completed** | Deposited to your account |

### Typical Timeline
- **Request submission**: Immediate
- **Processing**: 1-3 business days
- **Bank transfer**: 1-2 business days
- **Total**: 3-5 business days

## Payout History

View your payout history:
- All requested payouts
- Status of each request
- Amount and date
- Transaction reference

## Setting Up Payout Information

### Bank Account Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Access payout settings in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Bank Account">
    Enter your bank details:
    - Bank name
    - Account number
    - Account holder name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Complete any verification requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your bank account is now linked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Required Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Bank Name** | Your bank (e.g., SCB, Kasikorn, Bangkok Bank) |
| **Account Number** | Your account number |
| **Account Name** | Name on the account |
| **Branch** | Bank branch (optional) |

<Warning>
Ensure bank account details are accurate. Incorrect information causes delays and may result in failed transfers.
</Warning>

## Payout Fees

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| **Platform fee** | Usually none |
| **Bank transfer fee** | May vary by bank |
| **Minimum deduction** | Check current terms |

## Payout Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Waiting to be processed |
| **Scheduled** | Queued for next payout run |
| **Processing** | Being transferred |
| **Completed** | Successfully deposited |
| **Failed** | Transfer failed (contact support) |
| **Cancelled** | Request was cancelled |

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payout taking too long">
    - Check payout status in dashboard
    - Bank transfers can take 3-5 business days
    - Contact support if exceeds 7 days
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Payout failed">
    Common reasons:
    - Incorrect bank details
    - Account closed or frozen
    - Bank rejected transfer

    Fix details and request again, or contact support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't request payout">
    Check:
    - Balance meets minimum threshold
    - Bank account is set up
    - Account verification complete
    - No pending payout requests
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong amount received">
    - Review payout history for details
    - Check for any fees deducted
    - Contact support with transaction details
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
