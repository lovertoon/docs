## View Analytics

### Comic Views
Track how many times your content is viewed:
- Total views per comic
- Views per chapter
- View trends over time

### Chapter Views
Individual chapter performance:
- Unique viewers
- Repeat views
- Drop-off analysis

<Info>
High views but low purchases might indicate pricing issues. Low views but high purchases indicate engaged audience.
</Info>

## Revenue Analytics

### Revenue Trends
Visualize earnings over time:
- Daily/weekly/monthly charts

- Best-performing content

### Purchase Analysis
Understand buying behavior:
- Which chapters sell best

- Revenue per reader

## Audience Insights

### Follower Growth

### Engagement Metrics
How readers interact:
- Comment rates

- Return readers

## Performance Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric | What It Measures |
|--------|------------------|
| **Views** | Content discovery and reach |
| **Purchases** | Monetization success |
| **Conversion Rate** | Viewers who become buyers |
| **Comments** | Audience engagement |
| **Favorites** | Reader retention signals |

### Conversion Funnel
```
Visitors → Viewers → Free Readers → Purchasers → Repeat Buyers
```

Each step shows where you can improve.

## Using Analytics for Growth

### Identify Winners
Find your best-performing content:
- Which comics get most views?
- Which chapters sell best?
- What patterns emerge?

### Optimize Content
Use data to improve:
- Popular genres/themes
- Ideal chapter length

## Analytics Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check Regularly">
    Review analytics weekly to spot trends and issues early.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Look at Trends, Not Just Numbers">
    Is performance improving, stable, or declining? Direction matters more than absolute numbers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compare Like to Like">
    Compare similar chapters, not a prologue to a climax chapter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Don't Obsess">
    Analytics inform decisions but shouldn't paralyze creativity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Understanding Your Data

### Views vs. Unique Viewers
- **Views**: Total times content was accessed (includes repeats)
- **Unique Viewers**: Number of different people who viewed

### Revenue vs. Purchases
- **Revenue**: Total earnings (coin value)
- **Purchases**: Number of transactions

### Time-Based Patterns
Look for:
- Day of week trends
- Time of day trends
- Seasonal patterns
- Post-publish patterns

## Benchmarking

Compare your performance:

| Level | Typical Metrics |
|-------|-----------------|
| **New Creator** | Building initial audience |
| **Growing** | Consistent growth, loyal readers |
| **Established** | Stable revenue, large following |
| **Top Tier** | High volume, premium pricing |

<Tip>
Focus on improving your own metrics rather than comparing to others. Every creator's journey is different.
</Tip>

## Acting on Insights

### Low Views
- Improve cover images
- Optimize titles and descriptions
- Promote more actively
- Check category placement

### Low Conversion
- Review pricing strategy
- Improve chapter quality
- Add more free content
- Enhance chapter previews

### Low Engagement
- Respond to comments more
- Ask questions in your chapters
- Create community events
- Share behind-the-scenes
