## Creator Benefits

### Full Creative Control
- Publish comics or novels
- Set your own pricing
- Control chapter release schedule
- Mark content as adult if needed

### Revenue Sharing
- Earn from every chapter purchase
- Transparent earnings tracking
- Regular payout schedule
- Multiple withdrawal methods

### Marketing Features
- Free reading codes for promotion
- Creator PASS for Hot Stories
- Promotion bidding system
- Creator profile page

### Team Collaboration
- Invite assistants to help manage content
- Delegate tasks while maintaining control
- Team workflow support

## How Revenue Works

```
Reader Purchases Chapter
        ↓
Platform Processes Payment
        ↓
Revenue Split:
├── Creator: 85%
└── Platform: 15%
        ↓
Accumulated Earnings
        ↓
Payout (min. threshold)
```

<Note>
Revenue percentages may vary. See your creator agreement for exact terms.
</Note>

## Creator Dashboard

Once approved, you'll access the Creator Dashboard with:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview and analytics |
| **Comics** | Manage your comics and chapters |
| **Comments** | View and respond to reader comments |
| **Free Codes** | Create promotional codes |
| **Creator PASS** | Promote comics as Hot Stories |
| **Bidding** | Bid for homepage promotions |
| **Orders** | View purchase history |
| **Earnings** | Track revenue and payouts |
| **Assistants** | Manage team members |

## Requirements

### To Apply
- Active Lovertoon account
- Original work (no copyright infringement)
- Portfolio or sample work
- Commitment to regular updates

### To Maintain Status
- Follow platform guidelines
- Respond to reader engagement
- Maintain content quality
- No policy violations

## Content Guidelines

<Warning>
All content must be original or properly licensed. Copyright infringement will result in immediate removal and potential account termination.
</Warning>

### Allowed Content
- Original comics and novels
- Fan works (with proper disclaimers)
- Adult content (with proper marking)

### Prohibited Content
- Copyrighted material without permission
- Illegal content
- Content promoting harm
