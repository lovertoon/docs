## Comments Overview

The Comments section in your dashboard lets you:
- View all comments on your content
- Reply to readers
- Track engagement
- Identify popular content

## Accessing Comments

From your dashboard:
1. Click **ความคิดเห็น (Comments)** in the sidebar
2. View all comments across your comics

## Comment Dashboard

### Comment List
Each comment shows:
- Reader's name and avatar
- Comment text
- Comic and chapter info
- Timestamp
- Reply count

### Filtering Options

Filter comments by:
| Filter | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Comic** | View comments for specific comic |
| **Chapter** | View comments for specific chapter |
| **Date Range** | Comments from specific period |
| **Status** | Read/Unread comments |

### Sorting
Sort comments by:
- Newest first
- Oldest first
- Most replies

## Unread Indicator

The sidebar shows a badge with unread comment count. This helps you:
- Know when new comments arrive
- Prioritize responses
- Stay engaged with readers

## Replying to Comments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Comment">
    Locate the comment you want to reply to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Reply">
    Click the **Reply** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Response">
    Type your reply message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Send** or **Post** to publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reply Tips
- Be friendly and appreciative
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Acknowledge feedback (positive and constructive)
- Keep responses professional

## Marking as Read

Comments can be marked as read:
- Automatically when viewed
- Manually via checkbox
- Bulk mark as read

## Comment Notifications

You may receive notifications for:
- New comments on your chapters
- Replies to your replies
- Comment milestones

## Handling Different Comment Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Praise/Positive Comments">
    - Thank the reader
    - Express appreciation
    - Encourage continued reading
    - Consider liking the comment
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Constructive Criticism">
    - Thank for the feedback
    - Consider the validity
    - Explain your choices if appropriate
    - Apply feedback if useful
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Questions">
    - Answer promptly when possible
    - Be helpful without spoilers
    - Direct to relevant chapters if needed
    - Consider FAQ if common
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Inappropriate Comments">
    - Don't engage with trolls
    - Report if violates guidelines
    - Focus on positive community
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reporting Comments

If a comment violates guidelines:
1. Click the menu icon on the comment
2. Select **Report**
3. Choose the violation reason
4. Submit for moderator review

<Warning>
Platform moderators handle reported comments. Serious violations may result in user action.
</Warning>

## Building Community

Comments are opportunities to:
- Build reader loyalty
- Understand your audience
- Get story feedback
- Create fans, not just readers

### Engagement Strategies

| Strategy | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Quick responses** | Shows you care |
| **Personal touch** | Builds connection |
| **Ask questions** | Encourages discussion |
| **Tease upcoming content** | Builds anticipation |

## Comment Insights

Use comments to understand:
- Which parts readers love
- Confusing plot points
- Character popularity
- Desired story directions

<Tip>
Comments are free market research! Pay attention to patterns in feedback.
</Tip>

## Time Management

If you have many comments:
- Set a daily time for responses
- Prioritize questions and new readers
- Use saved replies for common responses
- Don't feel obligated to reply to everything
