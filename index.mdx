## About Lovertoon

Lovertoon is a Thai-language webtoon and novel reading platform that connects readers with talented creators. Whether you're looking to read your favorite comics, discover new stories, or share your own creations, Lovertoon has everything you need.

## Content Types

Lovertoon offers two main types of content:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Comics (การ์ตูน)** | Visual stories with manga-style artwork |
| **Novels (นิยาย)** | Text-based stories for immersive reading |

## Target Audiences

Content is categorized by target audience:

- **All** - Content suitable for all readers
- **Male** - Content primarily targeting male readers
- **Female** - Content primarily targeting female readers
- **BL/GL** - Boys' Love and Girls' Love content

<Note>
Some content may be marked as adult (18+). You must verify your age to access adult content.
</Note>
