## Automatic Progress Tracking

Lovertoon automatically tracks your reading activity:

| Tracked Data | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Chapters Viewed** | Every chapter you open |
| **Reading Progress** | Scroll position as a percentage |
| **Last Viewed Time** | When you last read each chapter |
| **View Count** | How many times you've read a chapter |

<Info>
Reading progress is tracked automatically as you scroll through chapters. No manual action required!
</Info>

## Accessing Reading History

### Profile Page
Navigate to `/profile` and view the **Recently Read** tab to see your reading history.

### Continue Reading
On comic detail pages, use the **Continue Reading** button to resume from your last position.

## Reading History Display

Each history entry shows:
- Comic cover image
- Comic title
- Chapter number and name
- Progress percentage
- Time since last read

### Progress Indicators

| Progress | Display |
|----------|---------|
| **0-25%** | Just started |
| **25-75%** | In progress |
| **75-100%** | Almost finished |
| **100%** | Completed |

## Features

### Resume Reading

When you return to a chapter you've started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chapter">
    Click on a chapter you've previously read.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resume Option">
    If you have progress saved, you can choose to resume from where you left off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue Reading">
    The page scrolls to your last position automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cross-Comic Progress

On the homepage, comics in your history may show:
- Which chapter you're on
- Reading progress indicator
- "Continue" button for quick access

### Comic Detail View

On each comic's page, chapters display:
- Checkmark if you've read/purchased the chapter
- Progress bar for partially read chapters
- "NEW" badge for chapters released since your last visit

## History Management

### Automatic History

Reading history is automatically maintained. No action needed to:
- Add new entries
- Update progress
- Track timestamps

### Privacy

Your reading history is:
- Private to your account
- Not visible to other users
- Not shared with creators

## Tips

  <Accordion title="Completing Chapters">
    Scroll to the very end of a chapter to mark it as 100% complete.
  </Accordion>
