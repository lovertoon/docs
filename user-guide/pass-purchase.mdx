## PASS Page

Access the PASS purchase page at `/pass` from:
- Homepage "Buy PASS" button (in Hot Stories section)
- Navigation menu
- Profile page

<Info>
You must be logged in to purchase a PASS.
</Info>

## Available Plans

### 30-Day PASS
| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Duration** | 30 days |
| **Benefit** | 50% off Hot Stories |
| **Best For** | First-time users, casual readers |

### 90-Day PASS
| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Duration** | 90 days |
| **Benefit** | 50% off Hot Stories |
| **Best For** | Regular readers, better value |

<Note>
Actual pricing may vary. Check the PASS page for current rates.
</Note>

## Purchase Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to PASS Page">
    Navigate to `/pass` or click "Buy PASS" from the Hot Stories section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Plan">
    Choose between 30-day or 90-day PASS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Purchase">
    Click the "Purchase" or "Buy Now" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment">
    Complete payment through Stripe (similar to coin top-up).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activation">
    PASS activates immediately upon successful payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Methods

PASS can be purchased using:
- Credit/Debit cards (via Stripe)
- Same payment methods as coin top-up

## After Purchase

### Immediate Activation
Your PASS becomes active immediately after successful payment.

### Confirmation
You'll see:
- Success message
- PASS expiration date
- Confirmation email (if enabled)

### Using Your PASS
1. Browse Hot Stories on the homepage
2. Click any Hot Story comic
3. When purchasing chapters, discount applies automatically
4. Pay the reduced price

## PASS Status

View your PASS status:

| Location | Information Shown |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Profile** | Active/Inactive, Expiration date |
| **PASS Page** | Full details, Renewal option |
| **Navigation** | PASS badge (if active) |

## Renewing Your PASS

Before expiration:
1. Go to PASS page
2. Purchase a new PASS
3. New duration **adds to** remaining time

After expiration:
1. Go to PASS page
2. Purchase a new PASS
3. Fresh subscription starts immediately

<Tip>
Renew before expiration to avoid any gap in your discount benefits!
</Tip>

## Extending Your PASS

You can purchase additional PASS time anytime:
- Buy while existing PASS is active
- Time is added (not replaced)
- No penalty for early renewal

Example:
```
Current PASS: 20 days remaining
Purchase: 30-day PASS
Result: 50 days total
```

## Cancellation & Refunds

<Warning>
PASS purchases are generally **non-refundable**.
</Warning>

PASS is a one-time purchase, not a recurring subscription. There's nothing to cancel.

For exceptional circumstances (accidental purchase, technical issues), contact support.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When does my PASS start?">
    Immediately upon successful payment. The countdown begins right away.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause my PASS?">
    No, PASS duration cannot be paused. Time continues counting down regardless of usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when PASS expires?">
    - Discounts stop applying
    - Hot Story chapters return to full price
    - Already purchased chapters remain unlocked
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is PASS automatically renewed?">
    No. PASS is a one-time purchase. You must manually purchase a new PASS when it expires.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I transfer PASS to another account?">
    No, PASS is non-transferable and tied to your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need coins too?">
    Yes! PASS provides discounts, but you still need coins to purchase chapters. PASS doesn't give free access.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
