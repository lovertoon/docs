## Accessing Chapters

### From Comic Detail Page
1. Navigate to the comic's detail page
2. Scroll down to the chapter list
3. Click on any chapter to start reading

### Quick Actions
- **Start Reading** - Opens the first chapter
- **Continue Reading** - Opens your last read chapter (with reading progress)

## Chapter Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free Chapters" icon="unlock">
    No purchase required. Usually the first 1-3 chapters of a comic are free to hook readers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Premium Chapters" icon="lock">
    Requires coins to unlock. Shows price with a coin icon.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Identifying Chapter Types

In the chapter list:
- **Free chapters** - No coin icon, click to read immediately
- **Premium chapters** - Shows coin icon with price (e.g., "5 coins")
- **Purchased chapters** - Shows checkmark, already unlocked

## Reading Interface

### Display Modes

Lovertoon supports different display modes depending on the comic:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Single Page Mode">
    Images are displayed vertically in a continuous scroll format. Perfect for webtoon-style comics designed to be read on mobile devices.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Paginated Mode">
    Images are displayed one at a time with page navigation. Traditional manga-style reading.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Reader Controls

#### Top Navigation Bar
| Control | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **Back Button** | Return to comic detail page |
| **Comic Title** | Shows current comic name |
| **Chapter Dropdown** | Quick navigation to other chapters |

#### Bottom Navigation Bar
| Control | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **Previous Chapter** | Go to the previous chapter |
| **Chapter List** | Open chapter selection |
| **Next Chapter** | Go to the next chapter |

### Reading Progress

Your reading progress is automatically saved:
- **Scroll position** - Where you stopped reading
- **Progress percentage** - How much of the chapter you've read
- **Last viewed time** - When you last read this chapter

<Info>
When you return to a chapter, you can choose to resume from where you left off or start from the beginning.
</Info>

## Purchasing Premium Chapters

When you click on a premium chapter you haven't purchased:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Purchase Dialog">
    A dialog appears showing:
    - Chapter title
    - Price in coins
    - Your current coin balance
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Purchase">
    Click **Purchase** to unlock the chapter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Reading">
    The chapter opens automatically after purchase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Insufficient Coins

If you don't have enough coins:
- A message shows your current balance
- A link directs you to the top-up page
- The purchase button is disabled

Consider getting a PASS subscription for 50% off on Hot Story chapters!
</Tip>

## Using Free Reading Codes

Creators can distribute free reading codes for their content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a Code">
    Obtain a free reading code from a creator (via social media, events, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Redeem">
    Click the **Redeem Code** button in the reader or purchase dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Code">
    Type or paste the code in the input field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock Content">
    If valid, the content is unlocked for free.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Code Types

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Chapter** | Unlocks a specific chapter |
| **Comic** | Unlocks all chapters of a specific comic |
| **All Creator Comics** | Unlocks all content from a creator |

<Warning>
Free reading codes have expiration times (1-72 hours) and can only be used once.
</Warning>

## Tips for Better Reading

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Reading on Mobile">
    - Use portrait mode for vertical scrolling comics
    - Pinch to zoom on detailed panels
    - Swipe left/right for chapter navigation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reading on Desktop">
    - Use keyboard arrows for navigation
    - Scroll with mousewheel or trackpad
    - Click edges of the screen to navigate
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Managing Reading List">
    - Add comics to favorites to find them easily
    - Check your reading history in profile
    - Use "Continue Reading" to pick up where you left off
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
