## Primary Benefits

### 50% Discount on Hot Stories

The main benefit of PASS is a **50% discount** on all Hot Story chapter purchases.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Without PASS" icon="coins">
    Chapter costs **5 coins** → You pay **5 coins**
  </Card>
  <Card title="With PASS" icon="ticket">
    Chapter costs **5 coins** → You pay **2.5 coins** (rounded)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### What Are Hot Stories?

Hot Stories are comics promoted by creators through the Creator PASS program. They appear:
- On the homepage in the "Hot Stories" section
- With a "HOT" badge
- With a countdown timer showing promotion duration

<Info>
Creators pay to have their comics featured as Hot Stories for 24-48 hours at a time. PASS holders benefit from discounts on this premium content.
</Info>

## How Discounts Apply

### Automatic Application
When you have an active PASS:
1. Navigate to any Hot Story chapter
2. The original price is shown crossed out
3. The discounted price (50% off) is displayed
4. Click purchase to pay the reduced amount

### Visual Indicators
- **Original Price**: ~~10 coins~~
- **PASS Price**: 5 coins
- **Savings**: 5 coins saved

## What PASS Doesn't Include

<Warning>
PASS does **not** provide:
- Free access to any content
- Discounts on regular (non-Hot Story) comics
- Ad-free reading (no ads currently)
- Early access to chapters
- Exclusive content
</Warning>

PASS is purely a discount program for Hot Stories.

## Maximizing PASS Value

### Check Hot Stories Daily
Hot Stories rotate frequently. Check the homepage regularly to find discounted content.

### Calculate Your Savings
Track how many Hot Story chapters you read per month. If the coins saved exceed the PASS cost, it's worth it.

### Plan Your Reading
Before buying PASS, browse Hot Stories to see if they interest you.

## Savings Scenarios

### Light Reader
- Reads 5 Hot Story chapters/month
- Average chapter price: 5 coins
- Without PASS: 25 coins/month
- With PASS: 12.5 coins/month
- **Monthly savings: 12.5 coins**

### Regular Reader
- Reads 20 Hot Story chapters/month
- Average chapter price: 5 coins
- Without PASS: 100 coins/month
- With PASS: 50 coins/month
- **Monthly savings: 50 coins**

### Heavy Reader
- Reads 50 Hot Story chapters/month
- Average chapter price: 5 coins
- Without PASS: 250 coins/month
- With PASS: 125 coins/month
- **Monthly savings: 125 coins**

<Tip>
Calculate your typical Hot Story reading to determine if PASS is cost-effective for you!
</Tip>

## PASS Holder Indicator

When logged in with an active PASS:
- Your profile shows a PASS badge
- Hot Story pages recognize your status automatically
- Discounted prices display without extra action

## Combining with Other Offers

### With Free Codes
- If you have a free reading code, use that first (it's free!)
- PASS doesn't stack with free codes (free is always better)

### With Promotions
- During platform-wide promotions, PASS discount may stack
- Check individual promotion terms

## Duration Benefits

### 30-Day PASS
- Good for trying out the service
- Ideal for specific reading periods
- Lower upfront cost

### 90-Day PASS
- Better value per day
- No need to remember to renew frequently
- Good for committed readers

## Creator Support

When you purchase Hot Story chapters with PASS:
- Creators still receive their revenue share
- The discount is subsidized by the PASS program
- You're actively supporting creator promotion efforts

<Info>
Buying PASS helps the creator ecosystem by making Hot Story promotions more attractive to creators!
</Info>
