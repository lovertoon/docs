## Homepage Sections

The Lovertoon homepage is your gateway to discovering great content. Here's what you'll find:

### Banner Carousel
<Info>
Featured content appears in a rotating carousel at the top of the page. This includes editor's picks, new releases, and promotional content.
</Info>

### Small Cover Promotions
Below the banner, you'll see promoted comics in a horizontal scroll. These are curated selections and bidding winners.

### Hot Stories Section
Comics promoted by creators who have joined the Creator PASS program. These comics offer:
- **50% discount** on chapter prices for PASS holders
- Limited-time promotions (24 hours each)
- Fresh recommendations from creators

### Trending Top 10
The most popular comics ranked by views. Filter by time period:

| Period | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Weekly** | Top comics from the past 7 days |
| **Monthly** | Top comics from the past 30 days |
| **All Time** | Overall most popular comics |

<Tip>
The top 3 comics get special badges: Gold (1st), Silver (2nd), and Bronze (3rd).
</Tip>

### Pinned Stories
Editor's pick comics that are permanently featured for their quality and popularity.

### Latest Updates
Comics with newly published chapters, organized by target audience tabs:
- **All** - All comics regardless of audience
- **Male** - Comics targeting male readers
- **Female** - Comics targeting female readers
- **BL/GL** - Boys' Love and Girls' Love content

## Comics Page

Visit `/comics` for the full comic browsing experience.

### Filtering Options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Category Filter">
    Filter by genre categories like:
    - Action
    - Romance
    - Fantasy
    - Comedy
    - Drama
    - Horror
    - Sci-Fi
    - And more...
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Status Filter">
    Filter by comic completion status:
    - **Ongoing** - Still being updated
    - **Completed** - Story is finished
    - **Hiatus** - Temporarily paused
    - **Dropped** - Discontinued
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Audience Filter">
    Filter by target audience:
    - Male
    - Female
    - BL/GL
    - All
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content Type">
    Switch between:
    - **Comics (การ์ตูน)** - Visual manga-style content
    - **Novels (นิยาย)** - Text-based stories
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Sorting Options

Sort comics by:
- **Latest Update** - Most recently updated first
- **Popularity** - Most viewed comics
- **Newest** - Recently added comics
- **Alphabetical** - A-Z by title

## Comic Detail Page

Click on any comic to view its detail page at `/comics/[slug]`.

### Information Displayed

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Cover Image** | High-quality comic cover |
| **Banner** | Optional banner image (displayed as background) |
| **Title** | Comic name in Thai |
| **English Title** | Optional English name |
| **Creator** | Link to the creator's profile |
| **Status** | Publication status badge |
| **Description** | Comic synopsis |
| **Target Audience** | Who the comic is for |
| **Categories** | Genre tags (clickable for filtering) |

### Action Buttons

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Start Reading" icon="play">
    Begin from the first chapter
  </Card>
  <Card title="Continue Reading" icon="forward">
    Resume from your last read chapter (if you have reading history)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add to Favorites" icon="heart">
    Save the comic to your bookshelf
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Chapter List

All chapters are listed with:
- Chapter number and title
- Publication date
- Price (free or coin amount)
- Purchase/read status
- Reading progress (if you've started reading)

## Creator Profiles

Visit `/creator/[username]` to see a creator's public profile:

- Creator display name and avatar
- Bio/description
- All published comics
- Follow button

<Tip>
Follow your favorite creators to see their comics more easily!
</Tip>

## Adult Content

<Warning>
Comics marked as 18+ require age verification. Adult content is hidden by default.
</Warning>

To view adult content:
1. Enable **Show Adult Content** in your profile settings
2. Confirm you are 18 years or older
3. Adult comics will now appear in your browsing

Adult comics display a red **18+** badge on their cover and detail page.
