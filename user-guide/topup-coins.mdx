## Accessing the Top Up Page

Navigate to the top-up page at `/topup` from:
- Profile page coin balance
- Navigation menu
- Purchase dialog when you don't have enough coins

<Info>
You must be logged in to purchase coins.
</Info>

## Payment Process

Lovertoon uses **Stripe** for secure payment processing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Package">
    Choose a coin package from the available options. Each package shows:
    - Number of coins
    - Price in Thai Baht (THB)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Purchase">
    Click the **Purchase** or **Buy** button on your selected package.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stripe Checkout">
    You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    Enter your payment information:
    - Card number
    - Expiry date
    - CVV
    - Cardholder name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Confirm your payment. You'll see a success message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Coins Added">
    Coins are automatically added to your account and you're redirected back to Lovertoon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accepted Payment Methods

Stripe supports various payment methods:

| Method | Supported |
|--------|-----------|
| Visa | Yes |
| Mastercard | Yes |
| American Express | Yes |
| JCB | Yes |

Available payment methods may vary by region. Stripe will show all options available to you.
</Note>

## Payment Status

After payment, you may see these statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Payment is being processed |
| **Completed** | Payment successful, coins added |
| **Failed** | Payment was declined |
| **Refunded** | Payment was refunded |

## Top-up History

View your purchase history:

1. Go to your Profile (`/profile`)
2. Click on **Top-up History** tab
3. See all your past purchases with:
   - Date and time
   - Package purchased
   - Amount paid
   - Payment status

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment was declined">
    Common reasons for declined payments:
    - Insufficient funds
    - Card expired
    - Incorrect card details
    - Bank security block (contact your bank)

    Try a different payment method or contact your bank.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Coins not appearing">
    If payment completed but coins don't appear:
    1. Wait a few minutes - sometimes there's a delay
    2. Refresh the page
    3. Log out and log back in
    4. Check your top-up history for payment status
    5. Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Duplicate charge">
    If you see a duplicate charge:
    1. Check your top-up history for duplicate entries
    2. Wait 24 hours - sometimes pending charges are released
    3. Contact support with your payment details
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Refund request">
    Coins are generally non-refundable. However, contact support if:
    - You were charged but didn't receive coins
    - Technical issues caused duplicate charges
    - Unauthorized purchase was made
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security

<Info>
Your payment information is handled securely by Stripe. Lovertoon never stores your card details.
</Info>

### Stripe Security Features
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- SSL/TLS encryption
- Fraud detection
- 3D Secure authentication (if enabled by your bank)
