## Creator Profiles

Every creator on Lovertoon has a public profile at `/creator/[username]` featuring:
- Display name and avatar
- Bio/description
- All published comics
- Follow button

## How to Follow a Creator

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Creator">
    Navigate to a creator's profile through:
    - Comic detail page (click on creator name)
    - Direct URL (`/creator/username`)
    - Search results
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Follow">
    Click the **Follow** button on their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done!">
    You're now following the creator.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
You must be logged in to follow creators.
</Info>

## Managing Follows

### Viewing Followed Creators
Access your followed creators from your profile or followers page.

### Unfollowing
To unfollow a creator:
1. Go to their profile
2. Click the **Following** button (now unfollow)
3. Confirm if prompted

## Creator Pages

### What You'll Find

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Profile Header** | Name, avatar, follower count |
| **Bio** | Creator's description |
| **Comics List** | All published works |
| **Social Links** | Links to other platforms |

### Discovering Comics
On a creator's profile:
- View all their comics
- See comic covers and titles
- Check chapter counts
- Quick links to comic pages

## Follower Counts

Creator profiles display:
- Total follower count
- This helps identify popular creators

<Tip>
Follower count isn't everything. Many talented new creators have great content worth discovering!
</Tip>

## Notifications

Currently, following a creator:
- Adds them to your follow list
- Makes it easier to find their content

<Note>
Direct notifications for new releases from followed creators may be added in future updates.
</Note>

## Finding Creators

### From Comic Pages
Every comic shows its creator. Click the creator name or avatar to visit their profile.

### From Hot Stories
Hot Stories display creator information. Tap to explore their other works.

### Through Search
Search for creator names to find their profiles directly.

## Supporting Creators

Beyond following, you can support creators by:
- Purchasing their premium chapters
- Commenting on their work
- Sharing their comics with friends
- Using PASS on their Hot Stories

<Info>
Creators earn revenue from chapter purchases. Every purchase directly supports their work!
</Info>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many creators can I follow?">
    There's no limit to how many creators you can follow.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can creators see who follows them?">
    Creators can see their follower count but may not see individual follower details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will creators know when I unfollow?">
    Unfollowing is private. Creators won't be notified.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
