## Profile Overview

Your profile page (`/profile`) displays:

- **Avatar** - Your profile picture
- **Display Name** - Your username shown to others
- **Email** - Your registered email address
- **Coin Balance** - Your current coin count

## Editing Your Profile

To update your profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Click your avatar in the top navigation and select **Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the **Edit Profile** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Information">
    You can update:
    - Display name
    - Profile picture (upload a new image)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Types

### Regular User
- Browse and read free content
- Purchase coins and unlock chapters
- Save favorites
- Comment on chapters

### Creator
- All user features
- Access to Creator Dashboard
- Publish comics and novels
- Earn revenue from content

### Creator Assistant
- Limited dashboard access
- Manage content on behalf of a creator
- Permissions set by the creator

## Authentication Methods

### Google Account
If you signed up with Google:
- Sign in with your Google account
- Profile picture syncs from Google
- Email verified automatically

### Email/Password
If you signed up with email:
- Sign in with email and password
- Can reset password via email
- Must verify email address

## Password Management

### Changing Password

For email accounts:
1. Go to your profile settings
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **Update Password**

### Forgot Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Sign In">
    Click **Sign In** in the navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Forgot Password">
    Click the **Forgot Password** link below the login form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Enter your registered email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    You'll receive a 6-digit OTP code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter OTP">
    Enter the OTP code on the verification page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Create a new secure password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Sections

### Favorites Tab
View all comics you've added to your bookshelf. Comics show:
- Cover image
- Title
- Latest chapter info
- Reading progress

### Reading History Tab
See your recently read chapters with:
- Comic title
- Chapter number
- Reading progress percentage
- Last read time

### Purchased Tab
View all chapters you've purchased:
- Comic and chapter details
- Purchase date
- Amount paid

### Top-up History Tab
Track your coin purchases:
- Package purchased
- Amount paid
- Payment status
- Date

## Adult Content Setting

<Warning>
Adult content (18+) is hidden by default. You must enable this setting to view adult comics.
</Warning>

To enable adult content:
1. Go to your profile
2. Toggle the **Show Adult Content** switch
3. Confirm you are 18 years or older

This setting is stored locally and resets if you clear your browser data.

## Signing Out

To sign out:
1. Click your avatar in the navigation
2. Select **Sign Out**
3. You'll be redirected to the homepage

<Tip>
Your reading progress and favorites are saved to your account, so you can continue where you left off on any device.
</Tip>
