## Commenting on Chapters

Each chapter has a comment section at the bottom where you can:
- Share your thoughts on the chapter
- Discuss story developments
- Connect with other readers
- Give feedback to creators

<Info>
You must be logged in to post comments.
</Info>

## Posting a Comment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read a Chapter">
    Navigate to any chapter you've read.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Comments">
    Find the comment section at the bottom of the chapter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Your Comment">
    Type your message in the comment box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Post** or **Send** to publish your comment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Comment Features

### Reply to Comments
Click the **Reply** button on any comment to respond directly. Replies are nested under the original comment.

### Like Comments
Show appreciation for a comment by clicking the like/heart button.

### View Replies
Expand nested replies to see the full conversation thread.

## Comment Guidelines

<Warning>
Comments that violate community guidelines may be removed. Repeated violations may result in account restrictions.
</Warning>

### Do's
- Share constructive feedback
- Discuss the story respectfully
- Support fellow readers
- Keep spoilers marked

### Don'ts
- Post spam or advertisements
- Use offensive language
- Harass other users or creators
- Share spoilers without warnings
- Post personal information

## Comment Moderation

### Creator Moderation
Creators can:
- View all comments on their content
- Reply to reader comments
- Report problematic comments
- Mark comments as read

### Admin Moderation
Platform administrators can:
- Review reported comments
- Remove inappropriate content
- Take action on violating accounts

## Notifications

Depending on settings, you may receive notifications for:
- Replies to your comments
- Likes on your comments
- Creator responses

## Comment Privacy

- Your display name appears on comments
- Your profile is visible to others
- Comments are public to all users who can access the chapter

<Tip>
Remember that comments are public. Be respectful and mindful of what you share.
</Tip>

## Deleting Comments

To delete your own comment:
1. Find your comment
2. Click the menu (⋮) icon
3. Select **Delete**
4. Confirm deletion

<Warning>
Deleted comments cannot be recovered. Replies to your comment will also be affected.
</Warning>

## Reporting Comments

To report an inappropriate comment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Comment">
    Locate the problematic comment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Report">
    Click the menu (⋮) icon and select **Report**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Reason">
    Choose the reason for reporting (spam, harassment, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit the report for review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Reports are reviewed by moderators who take appropriate action.

## Comment Statistics

Creators can view comment statistics in their dashboard:
- Total comments received
- Unread comments count
- Comments by chapter/comic
- Comment engagement trends

## Tips for Great Comments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be Constructive">
    Share specific feedback that helps creators improve. Mention what you liked and any suggestions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Avoid Spoilers">
    Use spoiler warnings if discussing major plot points. Not everyone reads at the same pace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Engage Respectfully">
    Different opinions are welcome. Discuss disagreements politely without personal attacks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Support Creators">
    A kind comment can make a creator's day. Share encouragement and appreciation!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see my comment?">
    Comments may take a moment to appear. Refresh the page. If still missing, it may have been flagged by moderation filters.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can creators see who commented?">
    Yes, creators see your display name on comments. They can respond to your comments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are comments anonymous?">
    No, your display name is visible on all comments you post.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
