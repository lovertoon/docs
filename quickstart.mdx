## Create Your Account

Lovertoon offers two ways to create an account:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Sign-In">
    1. Click **Sign In** in the top navigation
    2. Select **Continue with Google**
    3. Choose your Google account
    4. You're ready to start reading!
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Registration">
    1. Click **Sign Up** in the top navigation
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Create a secure password
    4. Enter your display name
    5. Click **Create Account**
    6. Verify your email if required
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Navigating the Homepage

The Lovertoon homepage features several sections:

### Banner Carousel
Featured comics and promotions displayed at the top of the page.

### Hot Stories
Comics promoted by creators with active PASS subscriptions. These offer 50% discounts for PASS holders.

### Trending Top 10
Most popular comics based on views:
- **Weekly** - This week's top comics
- **Monthly** - This month's top comics
- **All Time** - All-time most popular

### Latest Updates
Recently updated comics organized by target audience:
- All
- Male
- Female
- BL/GL

## Finding Content

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Browse Comics Page">
    Visit `/comics` to browse all available comics with filtering options:
    - **Category** - Filter by genre (Action, Romance, Fantasy, etc.)
    - **Status** - Ongoing, Completed, Hiatus, Dropped
    - **Target Audience** - Male, Female, BL/GL
    - **Content Type** - Comic or Novel
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Search">
    Use the search bar to find comics by:
    - Title (Thai or English)
    - Creator name
    - Category
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Creator Profiles">
    Visit creator profiles at `/creator/[username]` to see all their works.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reading Your First Comic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Comic">
    Browse or search for a comic you want to read.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Comic Details">
    Click on the comic cover to see:
    - Description
    - Categories
    - Creator info
    - Chapter list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Reading">
    Click **Start Reading** to begin from the first chapter, or select any chapter from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate Chapters">
    Use the navigation buttons to move between chapters:
    - Previous/Next chapter buttons
    - Chapter dropdown for quick navigation
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your Profile

Access your profile to manage:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Coin Balance** | View your current coin balance |
| **Favorites** | Comics you've saved to your bookshelf |
| **Reading History** | Recently read chapters with progress |
| **Purchased Chapters** | All chapters you've unlocked |
| **Top-up History** | Record of coin purchases |
